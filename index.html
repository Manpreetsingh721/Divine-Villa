About Divine Villa

Where the Sahyadri Hills Meet Quiet Luxury

Igatpuri surprises first-time visitors — the hills are greener, the air is cooler, and the pace is slower than the city you left behind. Divine Villa sits right in the middle of that calm, a private four-bedroom retreat built for families and friend groups who want the whole place to themselves.

Every stay includes a dedicated caretaker, a fully equipped kitchen, and an infinity-edge pool framed by the Western Ghats. No shared corridors, no fixed meal times — just your group, your schedule, and the mountains.
